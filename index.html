Hi there

I am a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India working on machine learning for tiny (really really really tiny) devices. I work primarily with Prateek, Manik, Harsha, Naga and others from our group.

We refer to the work that we are doing as our Embedded Devices Expedition. (In dark alleys and shadow economies, we have been referred to as EdgeML, Edge of Machine Learning and The Dank Group. Whispers have been heard.)

I am a huge Pink Floyd fan and as a whole, I am a `Systems` guy - I like to tinker around with embedded boards, micro-controllers, new tools and tricks and so forth. I am primarily driven by my curiosity about ideas loosely defined in cognitive science and that manifest, in some sense, in machine learning, deep learning and statistical inference.
