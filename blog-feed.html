Delhi: This is an experiment I'm doing with my blog. I am attempting to create a good looking design from ground up using simple CSS and JS, in stark contrast to what I used to do previously, developing by borrowing code from established websites. Though that made development really quick, I never understood completely what the code really did and that gaping hole of understanding remained.

This is my attempt towards acknowledging the complexities of developing a well designed, responsive and backward compatible website and the baby steps I am take towards understanding and appreciating it.
